Testing Standards & Review Ethics

  • Defined Use Case: We state the scenario and environment a tool is meant for.
  • Repeatable Metrics: We favor measurable outcomes (e.g., time-to-ignite, strikes-to-failure, run-time, mass/volume).
  • Transparent Limits: We disclose conditions, edge cases, and failure modes.
  • No Pay-for-Praise: We do not sell positive reviews. Samples may be returned, donated, or disclosed if retained.
