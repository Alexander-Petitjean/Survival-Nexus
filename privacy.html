Summary

We don’t sell personal data. We use limited technical information to run the site and improve content. If you choose to contact us, we’ll use your message to respond. Some pages may include affiliate links; those links do not give us your personal info.

Data We Collect

  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, pages viewed, timestamps, and referring URLs (standard web server logs).
  • Communication data: If you email us or submit a form, we receive the content you provide (e.g., name, email, message).
  • Cookies/Local Storage: Small files your browser may store to remember preferences (see Cookies below).

How We Use Data

  • Operate & secure the site (load pages, prevent abuse/fraud).
  • Improve content (understand what guides are helpful).
  • Respond to messages you send via email or forms.
  • Compliance with law, legal process, or enforce site policies.

Cookies & Similar Technologies

We use essential cookies for basic site functions (e.g., remembering navigation states). If we add non-essential cookies (e.g., analytics), we’ll document them here and, where required, request consent.

Do Not Track: We do not alter our practices when a browser sends a Do Not Track signal. You can control cookies via your browser settings.

Analytics

Currently: No third-party analytics are enabled by default.

Future (optional): If we add privacy-respecting analytics (e.g., self-hosted or IP-anonymized), we’ll update this policy with the provider’s name, what’s collected, and opt-out/consent options.

Affiliate Links

We may participate in affiliate programs (e.g., Amazon Associates, AvantLink). When you click an affiliate link, the retailer may use a cookie or tracking parameter to attribute any purchase to our site. We do not receive personal information about you from affiliate networks—only aggregated reports (e.g., item/category and commission).

See also our Disclaimer for full affiliate disclosure and review standards.

When We Share Data

  • Service providers: If used, only to operate the site (e.g., hosting) under confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal reasons: To comply with law, regulation, or valid legal request; to protect rights, safety, or property.
  • Business changes: If we reorganize or transfer the site, data may transfer subject to this policy.

Data Retention

We keep server logs for a limited period for security and diagnostics. If you contact us, we retain messages as long as needed to respond and maintain records, then delete when no longer necessary.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Use caution when sharing sensitive information online.

Your Rights & Choices

Your privacy rights depend on your location. Where applicable (e.g., California/EEA), you may request to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold, or object to/limit certain processing.

  • Access/Correction/Deletion: Contact us and we’ll assist when we can verify your request.
  • Opt-out of marketing: We don’t send marketing email by default; if we ever do, you’ll have an unsubscribe option.
  • Cookies: Control or delete via your browser settings.

We do not sell personal information. We do not knowingly process sensitive categories.

Children

This site is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our site or laws change. The “Last updated” date reflects the latest revision.
