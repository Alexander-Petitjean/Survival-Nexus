We’re transparent about how we fund Survival Nexus and the products we recommend.

Some links on Survival Nexus are affiliate links. If you click a link and purchase a product, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. These commissions help us maintain the site and fund field testing.

As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases. We only link to items we believe are reliable for field use.

4. Editorial Independence
